TouhouSheetMusic
=====================
Piano arrangements of the music of the popular doujin game Touhou Project

###Why Lilypond?
Because

+ I am tired of Finale and Musescore's notation method (or maybe I am just too lazy)
+ I am a programmer
+ Lilypond is perfect for version controlling
